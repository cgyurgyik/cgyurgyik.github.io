[Calyx](https://capra.cs.cornell.edu/calyx/) is a new intermediate language (IL) for compiling high-level programs into hardware designs. It combines hardware-like structural language with a software-like control flow representation with loops and conditionals. This talk will focus on the motivation behind Calyx, what it is today, and progress on a current frontend, namely TVM Relay.
