Hi! I am a 4th year student at Cornell University, and currently serve as a Research Assistant under [Adrian Sampson](https://www.cs.cornell.edu/~asampson/). My work primarily focuses on the development of frontends for [Calyx](https://capra.cs.cornell.edu/calyx/), a compiler infrastructure for languages that target hardware accelerators. 

Prior to arriving in Ithaca, I served in the United States Marine Corps for 4 years with 1st Battalion, 12th Marines. I am a strong advocate for military veterans attending regionally accredited, non-profit universities. In my free time, I enjoy speaking French, following the development of the C++ language, and a wide variety of rock climbing.
