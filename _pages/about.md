Hi!

I recently finished my undergraduate degree at Cornell University, and worked as a Research Assistant under [Adrian Sampson](https://www.cs.cornell.edu/~asampson/). My work primarily focuses on [Calyx](https://capra.cs.cornell.edu/calyx/), a compiler infrastructure for languages that target hardware accelerators, and [Dahlia](https://capra.cs.cornell.edu/fuse/docs/overview/), a programming language for designing hardware accelerators. You can reach me at: cpg49 (at) cornell (dot) edu.

Prior to arriving in Ithaca, I served in the United States Marine Corps for 4 years with 1st Battalion, 12th Marines. I am a strong advocate for military veterans attending regionally accredited, non-profit universities. In my free time, I enjoy a wide variety of rock climbing, following the development of the C++ language, and speaking French.
